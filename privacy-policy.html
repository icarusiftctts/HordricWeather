HordricWeather

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 8, 2025 | Version 1.0.0

1. Introduction

HordricWeather (hereinafter "the Application") is a weather forecasting mobile application developed by HordRicJr. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

By using the Application, you agree to the practices described in this privacy policy.

2. Information Collected

2.1 Location Data

The Application collects your geographical location to provide accurate and personalized weather forecasts. This data includes:

  • GPS coordinates (latitude, longitude)
  • City or region name
  • Local time zone
Legal basis: Explicit consent upon first use of the Application.
Usage: Location data is used solely to query the OpenWeather API.
Retention: The last known position is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

2.2 User Preferences

The Application stores the following information locally:

  • Username (if provided)
  • Saved favorite cities
  • Notification settings (enabled/disabled)
  • Display preferences (units of measurement, language)

Retention: This data is stored exclusively on your device via Android's local storage system (SharedPreferences).

2.3 Weather Data

The Application retrieves weather data via the OpenWeather API, including:

  • Temperature, humidity, atmospheric pressure
  • Weather conditions (rain, snow, clear sky, etc.)
  • Wind speed and direction
  • Hourly and daily forecasts
  • Air Quality Index (AQI)
  • Atmospheric pollutant levels (PM2.5, PM10, NO2, O3, SO2, CO)

Source: OpenWeather (https://openweathermap.org/)

Retention: Weather data is cached locally for 30 minutes.

3. Data Usage

The collected data is used exclusively to:

  • Display weather forecasts for your current location
  • Provide forecasts for cities you have added to your favorites
  • Send relevant weather notifications (alerts, changes, forecasts)
  • Display air quality and associated health advice
  • Provide weather-appropriate clothing recommendations
  • Update the weather widget on your home screen
Important: No data is used for advertising, commercial purposes, or profiling.

4. Data Sharing

4.1 Third-Party Services

The Application only communicates with the following services:

OpenWeather API
• URL: https://api.openweathermap.org/
• Data transmitted: GPS coordinates (latitude, longitude)
• Purpose: Retrieving weather data
• Privacy policy: https://openweather.co.uk/privacy-policy

4.2 No Proprietary Servers

The Application does not have proprietary backend servers. All your personal data (name, favorite cities, preferences) is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

4.3 No Data Sale

We do not sell, rent, or exchange your personal data with third parties for commercial purposes.

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Local Storage

All your personal data is stored locally on your Android device via:

  • SharedPreferences (user preferences, last location)
  • Application cache (temporary weather data)

No data is stored on remote servers.

5.2 Security Measures

We implement the following security measures:

  • Encrypted communications via HTTPS with the OpenWeather API
  • Secure storage in the Android sandbox
  • No transmission of sensitive data to third parties
  • Regular dependency updates to fix vulnerabilities

6. Android Permissions

The Application requires the following permissions:

Permission Purpose Required
ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION Obtain your precise GPS location Yes
ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION Obtain your approximate location Yes
ACCESS_BACKGROUND_LOCATION Update weather in the background No*
INTERNET Retrieve weather data Yes
POST_NOTIFICATIONS Send weather alerts No*
WAKE_LOCK Keep device active for updates Yes
RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED Restart updates after reboot Yes

*No = You can deny these permissions, but some features will be disabled.

7. Your Rights

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable laws, you have the following rights:

7.1 Right of Access

You can view all your data stored in the Application via the Settings menu.

7.2 Right to Rectification

You can modify your username, favorite cities, and preferences at any time in the settings.

7.3 Right to Erasure

You can delete all your data in three ways:

  1. In the Application: Menu → Settings → Reset Data
  2. Via Android: Settings → Apps → HordricWeather → Storage → Clear Data
  3. Uninstallation: Uninstalling automatically deletes all your local data

8. Notifications

The Application may send the following types of notifications:

  • Extreme weather alerts (heatwave, extreme cold, storms, high winds)
  • Significant weather changes (temperature variation >5°C)
  • Hourly forecasts (notifications of imminent rain, snow, or storms)
  • Daily weather (morning summary at 8:00 AM)
  • Lockscreen weather (every 15 minutes)

You can disable all notifications in: Menu → Settings → Notifications

9. Applicable Legislation

This privacy policy complies with the following regulations:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) - European Union
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act) - United States
  • Applicable local laws according to your country of residence
